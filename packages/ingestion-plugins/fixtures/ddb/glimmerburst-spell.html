Glimmerburst

An invented cantrip that exists only inside this test fixture. It produces harmless sparkles and mild delight.

Spell Details

Level Cantrip
Casting Time 1 action
Range 30 feet
Components V, S, a pinch of glitter
Duration Instantaneous

A burst of imaginary glitter erupts at a point you choose. Creatures in the area are briefly charmed by how pretty it is. No real game mechanics were harmed in the making of this paragraph.
