Grumble the Gremlin

Grumble is an entirely made-up creature invented for this repository's test fixtures. It hoards bottle caps and fears the color orange. Nothing on this page comes from any real rulebook.

Grumble's Lair

The lair is a drainpipe under an imaginary bridge. Visitors report a strong smell of pennies and old rain. The gremlin rearranges its bottle-cap collection nightly, sorting by shininess and then by sentimental value.

Persistent legends claim the drainpipe connects to a network of tunnels, but every mapping expedition has returned with nothing except mild colds and strong opinions about drainage infrastructure.

Stat Block

Grumble the Gremlin

Tiny fabrication, chaotically tidy

Armor Class 13 (bottle-cap mail)

Hit Points 7 (2d4 + 2)

Speed 25 ft., scurry 40 ft.

Bottle Cap Toss. Ranged attack: +4 to hit, range 20 ft., one target. Hit: 1 shiny distraction.

Tidy Panic. When a creature disturbs the collection, Grumble must reorganize it before taking any other action.

Disposition

Grumble is not hostile, merely territorial about alphabetization. Offerings of unbent paperclips grant advantage on negotiation, which in this synthetic universe means everyone simply gets along.
